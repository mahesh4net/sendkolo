FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I have to use Sendkolo?

SendKolo simplifies the process of collecting and transferring money to Africa, providing a user-friendly platform that ensures your contributions reach their intended recipients quickly and efficiently.

How is Sendkolo different from other solutions ?

Unlike many platforms, SendKolo focuses solely on supporting African communities, offering lower fees and faster transactions while maintaining transparency and excellent customer service.

How does Sendkolo make profitwithout charging transfer fees?

SendKolo partners with local organizations and businesses to facilitate transfers, earning revenue through strategic collaborations and sponsorships, allowing us to offer no-fee transfers for users.

Why do I have to use Sendkolo?

By using SendKolo, you’re not just sending money; you’re contributing to sustainable development in Africa. Your support helps empower communities and fosters economic growth.

How does Sendkolo ensure the security and reliability of transfers?

SendKolo employs advanced encryption and complies with international financial regulations to ensure every transaction is secure, reliable, and transparent, giving you peace of mind with your contributions.
